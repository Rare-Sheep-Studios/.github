# 🐑 Rare Sheep Studios

Welcome to **Rare Sheep Studios**! We're a small but chaotic collective of developers, artists, writers, and weirdos who make games, tools, and experimental projects. Most of our projects live here, some are open-source, and some are just shared for internal magic. 

---

## What We Do

- **Game Development:** From large-scale projects like *ELRB* to experimental chaos like *Wool & Dice*.
- **Open Source & Tools:** We share code, utilities, and frameworks to make development easier (or more ridiculous) for everyone.
- **Community Chaos:** We sometimes host sites, servers, or experimental web stuff just to see if it blows up. (Metaphorically… mostly.)

---

## Projects (Public-Friendly Overview)

- **ELRB** – Our main ongoing research facility game. Think science, danger, and slightly questionable decisions.
- **Holdout** – A.K.A The L4D2 wannabe.
- **Skybreak** – Top-Down camera colony builder, inspired by Helldivers 2, Interstellar, and more...
- **FRACTURED** – Pyshogical horror, or something... yeah.
- **Wool & Dice** – Self-hosted DnD-style fun; modular and open for tinkering.
- **Other Projects** – Some things are in super early chaos stage, others we forgot exist, but hey, that’s part of the fun.

## How to Use This Organization

1. **Developers:** Fork, clone, and collaborate! Most repos are shared for internal development, but pull requests and contributions are welcome for open-source projects.
2. **Gamers & Curious Folks:** Check out the open-source projects like *Wool & Dice* or other tools. Self-host, tweak, or break things—your call.
3. **Everyone Else:** Watch us for updates, chaos, and occasional project releases.
